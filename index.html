Instructions:
  1. Start timer.
  2. When "FOCUS" is displayed, work on your task.
  3. When "BREAK" is displayed, take a short break.
  4. Repeat steps 2 and 3 while you have less than or equal to four tomatoes.
  5. When "REST" is displayed, take a longer break.
  6. Repeat from step 1.

Default values for Focus, Break and Rest intervals are 25, 5, and 15 minutes respectively. You can change settings in the SETTINGS menu in the top right corner of the screen.
